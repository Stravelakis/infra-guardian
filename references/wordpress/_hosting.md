# WordPress Hosting

## In plain English
Where my WordPress sites actually live. Different hosts give me
different control panels and levels of control.

## What I use
| Host        | Panel    | Notes                              |
|-------------|----------|------------------------------------|
| Hostinger   | hPanel   | Managed shared hosting             |
| AsuraHosting| cPanel   | Traditional cPanel hosting         |
| Self-hosted | aaPanel  | My own VPS, full control           |

## Good to know
- Keep regular backups before major changes (see backups note).
- LiteSpeed-based hosts pair well with the LiteSpeed Cache plugin.
- Database access differs per panel — check the specific panel docs.
