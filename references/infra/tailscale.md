# Tailscale

## In plain English
A zero-config VPN that connects all my devices into one private network
as if they were sitting next to each other — even across the internet.
No port forwarding, no complex firewall rules.

## Where I use it
- Reaching servers and self-hosted apps privately from anywhere
- Connecting my laptop, phone, and servers into one secure mesh

## Good to know
- Built on WireGuard; connections are end-to-end encrypted.
- Each device gets a stable private IP on the "tailnet".
- Great alternative/companion to Cloudflare Tunnel for private access.
